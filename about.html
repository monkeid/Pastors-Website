About Us

My passion is to have healthy leading churches for the global glory of God. We’ve put together a flowing stream of content to nourish and enrich the lives and leadership of young generation everywhere. We want this space to be a dynamic community where church leaders can draw encouragement and wisdom from one another.

Mission

Building People who build Community

Vision

Raising A People Of Integrity ,Honor And Attraction

About Ben Okello, Founder

Ben Okello is an International Conference and Crusade speaker, and the author of Seed of Greatness, Spirit of Conquest, among other books.

He has travelled widely across Africa, South America and Europe addressing critical issues affecting people's social and spiritual development.

He is the founder and senior pastor of Mountain of Solutions and Answers Cathedral in Homabay (Kenya). He is the African President of VOHM and the Director of Mosaic School of Ministry. He is a radio host, a frequent guest to many TV stations, a graduate of EASM, Word of Faith Bible College, Dr. Gorman School of Leadership,

Reinhard Bonnke School of Fire and a holder of a doctorate in Christian Leadership. Pastor Ben and his wife,

Pastor Christine are proud parents to Brian, Ammi, Ruhama, Sheril and Randel Bloom among other adopted children.

About Addah Christine, Co-Founder

She has been invited to speak at the United Nations, the World Economic Forum in Davos, the African Union, the Council on Foreign Relations, Harvard’s Kennedy School of Government, TIME’s Global Health Summit, and numerous congresses around the world. TIME magazine named him one of “15 World Leaders Who Mattered Most in 2004” and in 2005 one of the “100 Most Influential People in the World.” Also, in 2005 U.S. News & World Report named him one of “America’s 25 Best Leaders.”

She as lectured at Oxford, Cambridge, the University of Judaism, the Evangelical Theological Society, and numerous seminaries and universities. His six books are known for explaining theology in understandable ways and have been translated into more than 50 languages. She teaches theology without using theological terms and telling people it is theology. His latest book, , has sold 25 million copies and is one of the best-selling nonfiction books in publishing history, with more than 50 million copies sold in all formats.

Ben Okello's Ministry Toolbox

All leaders are learners. As soon as you stop learning, you stop leading.

The Ministry Toolbox is designed to help you learn from 24+ years in ministry. It’s a weekly shot in the arm of wisdom that will help you develop and deepen your passion for ministry and ability to serve others.
