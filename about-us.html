More About Ben.

Pastor BenOkello

He has travelled widely across Africa, South America and Europe addressing critical issues affecting people's social and spiritual development. He is the founder and senior pastor of Mountain of Solutions and Answers Cathedral in Homabay (Kenya). He is the African President of VOHM and the Director of Mosaic School of Ministry. He is a radio host, a frequent guest to many TV stations, a graduate of EASM, Word of Faith Bible College, Dr. Gorman School of Leadership, Reinhard Bonnke School of Fire and a holder of a doctorate in Christian Leadership. Pastor Ben and his wife, Pastor Christine are proud parents to Brian, Ammi, Ruhama, Sheril and Randel Bloom among other adopted children.
